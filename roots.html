At the young age of 22, Lil Danny is already making waves in the music world.

A Toronto native, Lil Danny grew up in a family of music lovers. He was graced by listening to iconic musicians like Miles Davis, James Brown, and Ray Charles.

These influencers encouraged and inspired Lil Danny to pursue his talents and dreams. He is a recent graduate from Humber College’s Jazz program.

Shortly thereafter his time at Humber, he released his debut album, The Call. Lil Danny’s first album is a unique fusion of classical jazz and contemporary hip hop. Lil Danny’s been sharing his sound, touring The Call across Canada and the US for the past two years. Lil Danny has touched down at music festivals too, including Bonnaroo, Coachella, and Lollapalooza, connecting with fans along the way.

Lil Danny is working on his second album, Skull, which has darker, angstier vibes. Skull is slated for release in early spring.
